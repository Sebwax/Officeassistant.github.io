About

Learn more about Office assistant

Its simply a group of Professional Freelancers

Office assistant is a newly introduced online platform in Uganda which is intended to provide support to office workers, students, companies and many other parties that may need the services we offer. The platform is to act as your assistant if you accept to have it with you at your work place/Office in that you will be telling us where you need support and our team will always be set to help you. We shall be in position to provid you with all kind of support you need, be it advice or even to do some work on your behalf.

Office assistant is a network that is to provide employment to skilled persons in various sectors and this work can be done anywhere at any time. Ssebwana Mike, the founder is calling upon all the skilled and none skilled persons to join this platform which will currently operate through whatsapp as we are improving everything together. All you need is a computer, internet, and Whatsapp for proper communication.
